# ATM Withdrawal Limits (General Guide)

ATM withdrawal limits are daily cash withdrawal caps set by banks to reduce fraud risk and manage cash availability.

## What is an ATM withdrawal limit?
An ATM withdrawal limit is the maximum amount of cash you can withdraw from an ATM within a 24-hour period.

Limits vary based on:
- Your bank
- Your account type
- Your debit card settings
- Whether the ATM is in-network

## Why do banks set limits?
Banks use withdrawal limits to:
- Prevent large fraudulent withdrawals
- Protect customer accounts
- Control cash supply at machines

## How to check your limit
Most banks allow you to view your ATM limit inside:
- Mobile banking apps
- Online banking settings
- Customer service support
